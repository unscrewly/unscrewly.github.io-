Zero. We handle 100% of the technical side. You just tell us the problem in plain language — we do the rest. We also train you fully before handing over.

Most standard automations (WhatsApp bot, lead alerts) go live within 48–72 hours after the first call. Larger custom projects may take 5–7 days.
What if something breaks?
WhatsApp us and we'll fix it. Fast. For Growth and Business plans, we guarantee a response within 4 hours. For Starter, within 24 hours. We take reliability seriously.

Yes. No hidden setup fees, no per-message charges from us. Third-party tools (like BotPenguin) may have their own free tiers which we'll help you use. We're transparent about all costs upfront.

Absolutely. Everything is done remotely over WhatsApp and video calls. We serve businesses all across India. Location doesn't matter — your problem does.

If the automation doesn't work as agreed within 7 days of launch, you get a full refund. We believe in the work we do — and you shouldn't pay for something that doesn't deliver.
